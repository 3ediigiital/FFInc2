About GlobalFresh

GlobalFresh Produce Ltd is a specialist IQF producer and exporter based in Venlo, Netherlands — Europe's largest fresh produce hub. Founded in 2008, we supply bulk frozen fruits and vegetables to food service operators, retail chains, and food manufacturing clients across 38 countries.

Our 18,000 m² production facility processes over 45,000 MT annually, operating to the highest food safety standards with full cold chain traceability from farm to container.

Why Choose GlobalFresh
Low minimum orders — from 1 pallet, suitable for importers of all sizes
Full organic range — EU and USDA certified organic across 18 product lines
Custom packaging — private label and retail-ready pack formats available
Year-round supply — multi-origin sourcing ensures consistent availability
Dedicated export team — experienced staff covering all major import markets
